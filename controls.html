Controls

  • Player is two blocks tall and collides with solid blocks.
  • Survival has gravity and no flying; Creative has flying and instant break.
  • Look up/down: selector follows crosshair correctly.
  • Selected block or entity turns white.
  • Entities are built from 3D block-like parts. If selected, each model block turns white.
  • Left click / mobile Attack: attacks selected entity or breaks selected block.
  • Right click / mobile Use: opens doors/trapdoors/chests/beds/farmland or places blocks.
  • Q drops selected hotbar item. Walk over drops to pick up.
